# Diarrhea and Dehydration Protocol Notes

Source: Demonstration dehydration packet. Replace with official national child-health protocol before real use.

For diarrhea, assess duration, blood in stool, vomiting, thirst, ability to drink, sunken eyes, skin pinch, lethargy, and signs of severe dehydration.

Severe dehydration signs or lethargy require urgent referral and rehydration according to local protocol.

If no severe signs are present, counsel on fluids, ORS where appropriate, feeding, danger signs, and follow-up according to the protocol.
